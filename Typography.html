Typography is the art and technique of arranging text to make written language clear, readable, and visually appealing when displayed. It involves the selection of: Typefaces (fonts)choosing styles like serif, sans-serif, script, etc. Font size, spacing, and alignment, controlling how letters, words, and lines are spaced and positioned. Visual hierarchy emphasizing important information through size, weight, or color differences. In short, typography is about how text looks and feels in a design, not just what it says. It plays a key role in communication, branding, and creating mood or tone in any visualwork.
